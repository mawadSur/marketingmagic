## 1. The reframe: what "10X" should actually mean

The 06-20 doc's own retro is right: 10X-ing *cold DMs to marquee founders* (Levels, Marc Lou, Arvid) = 10X of ~0% reply rate. They have audiences and entrenched tooling; a cold design-partner ask is low-yield.

**The real 10X is not 100 cold DMs. It is a multi-channel, warm-first, repeatable machine** that compounds:

| Lever | 06-20 (before) | 10X (now) |
|---|---|---|
| **Volume** | 10 targets | 100+ targets, sourced **live** (proven engine, §4) |
| **Segment** | Marquee (low yield) | **Mid-tier 1k–20k #buildinpublic builders posting this week** (high yield) |
| **Channels** | X DM only | X DM **after engaging** + X content engine + IndieHackers/Reddit/communities + cold email |
| **Warmth** | Cold | **Warm**: like/reply on their post 1–2 days before the DM |
| **Repeatable** | One-shot doc | A daily 30–45 min cadence + a live-sourcing playbook + a tracker |
| **Inbound** | None | A **build-in-public content engine** so prospects come to us |

Yield math (illustrative, to set expectations, not promises): 100 warm DMs → ~25–40% reply on the mid-tier segment (vs ~2–5% cold-marquee) → ~15–25 conversations → ~8–12 try it → **~3–6 activated design partners**. That's the realistic first-customer base. Track the funnel (§6) and tune; don't trust these numbers, replace them with real ones after week 1.

> **Alex honesty flag (sequencing):** we have **0 activation data** and the channel-connect step is historically our cliff ([[activation-funnel-fix]]). Pouring 100 prospects into a funnel we haven't proven converts is risky. The hedge baked into this plan: start with a **20-DM warm batch** as a probe (week 1), watch reply → signup → *channel-connected* → *first post published*. Only scale to 100+ once a design partner actually reaches "published." If they stall at connect, the highest-leverage work flips from acquisition to fixing activation — I'll call that the moment we see it.

## 2. The four plays (run in parallel)

### Play A — X warm outbound (the core)
The highest-yield channel for this ICP. **Never cold-DM.** Sequence per target:
1. **Day 0:** like + leave one *genuine, specific* reply on their most recent build-in-public post (value, not a pitch). (Reply templates in `outreach-10x-templates`.)
2. **Day 1–2:** send the personalized DM (free-Pro design-partner ask, referencing the post you engaged with).
3. **Day 5:** one soft follow-up if no reply, then drop it.
- **Target volume:** 10 new warm DMs/day = ~50/week. Cap per X's DM limits (§7).

### Play B — X build-in-public content engine (inbound)
We sell a build-in-public tool — so **build marketingmagic in public**, dogfooding our own product to post about building it. This is our single most credible proof and turns outbound into inbound. 10 ready post templates in `outreach-10x-templates`. **Cadence: 1 post/day** about what we shipped (the dedup gate, the mobile nav, the "coming soon" video cutter, the learning loop), each ending with a soft "I'm building this to fix X — DM me if you want in." Dogfood: schedule these *through marketingmagic itself* (proof we use it).

### Play C — Community value-posts
Where build-in-public founders already gather. **Value-first, never a drive-by link.**
- **IndieHackers** — a "build-in-public, but I automated my own distribution" post + milestone updates.
- **Reddit** — r/SaaS, r/indiehackers, r/EntrepreneurRideAlong (read each sub's self-promo rules first; lead with the lesson, mention the tool once).
- **Build-in-public communities** — WIP (wip.co), the X "Build in Public" community (id 1493446837214187523, seen live today), relevant Discords/Slacks.
- **Cadence:** 2–3 value posts/week. Templates in `outreach-10x-templates`.

### Play D — Cold email (where findable)
For founders whose email is on their site/GitHub. Lower-priority than X for this ICP but scalable. 3-touch sequence in `outreach-10x-templates`. **Volume:** 10–15/day max from a warmed inbox (§7). Personalize line 1 from their site/product.

## 4. Live-sourcing engine (PROVEN today, 2026-06-22)

The 06-20 blocker — "web fetch can't read live X, Nitter is dead" — is solved: driving a logged-in Chrome reads live X cleanly. Verified today by harvesting `#buildinpublic` Latest. **This is the repeatable method to go from a seed list to 100+:**

**Queries (rotate; X search → "Latest"):**
- `#buildinpublic` · `#buildinginpublic` · `#indiehackers` · `#indiehacker`
- `"building in public"` · `"my first SaaS"` · `"day 1 of building"` / `"day N of building"`
- `"launched" #buildinpublic` · `#SaaS #buildinpublic`
- Pain-intent: `"posting is hard"`, `"hate marketing"`, `"no time for content"`, `"growing on X is hard"` (these surface the exact prospect mid-complaint)
- The **People** tab of `#buildinpublic` (surfaces dedicated BIP accounts) + the X "Build in Public" Community feed.

**Harvest method (what I did):** open the query → "Latest" → read the page's accessibility tree (gives handle + display name + post text + view/like counts per tweet) → scroll → repeat. ~5–8 candidates per screen.

**ICP filter (keep / drop):**
- ✅ KEEP: solo/small-team building a **SaaS/app/tool**, English (or a language you DM in), **1k–20k followers**, posting build-in-public *this week*, low-ish engagement (they want eyeballs), no in-house marketing.
- ❌ DROP: pure game-asset/crypto/AI-spam accounts, big accounts (>50k, won't reply), dormant accounts, agencies, anything off-ICP.

**Scale target:** 15 min/day of this = ~20–30 qualified targets/day → 100+ within a week, all live and current. (Seed batch already harvested → `outreach-10x-targets`.)

## 5. Tracker (set this up first)

A simple sheet (Google Sheet / Airtable / a CSV) is the system of record. Columns:

`handle | name | source(query) | followers | their_recent_post | why_fit | channel(X/email/IH) | engaged_on(date) | dm_sent(date) | reply(Y/N) | status | next_action | notes`

**Status values:** `queued → engaged → dm_sent → replied → trying → activated(published) → passed`.
The funnel report (§6) is just counts of each status. Keep it brutally honest.

## 6. Metrics (leading indicators — instrument, don't guess)

- **Activity:** engages/day, DMs/day, emails/day, content posts/day. (Did we do the work?)
- **Response:** reply rate per channel/segment. (Is the message landing?)
- **Conversion:** replies → signups → **channel-connected** → **first post published** (= activated = North Star unit).
- **The number that matters:** count of **activated design partners** (connected + published weekly). First milestone: **3**. Then **10**.
- Cross-check signups in `/admin/metrics` (the WAPW/activation funnel we already built, [[activation-funnel-fix]]) so outreach ties to real product behavior, not vanity replies.
